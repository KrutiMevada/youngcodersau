Innovation challenge

Are you excited by new ideas to design, code and stay connected?

Learn how to create, code and market your own digital sticker (used in mobile messaging) with VU’s innovation challenge.

Entry is open to Year 10, 11 and 12 students in secondary schools across Victoria.


30th June to 8th July, 2018

Why This Event Is Super Cool?

Masterclass for FREE

Attend an interactive MasterLab workshop and learn how to create a sticker using Swift coding language.
Workshops are run by VU in conjunction with Young Coders AU and Design Consulting.
Your school's stickers will then be published under Victoria University on the App store. The sticker with the most downloads at the end of the competition wins a prize.

Location & dates

MasterLab workshops are held at the Footscray Nicholson Campus. Students can choose one workshop from the following dates.

9am - 3pm, Saturday 30 June
9am - 3pm, Sunday 1 July
9am - 3pm, Saturday 7 July
9am - 3pm, Sunday 8 July.

Awards ceremony

Winners will be announced at the College of Business hosted awards ceremony at Open Day on Sunday 20 August 2018. Prize details to come.
